Why We Run

Philosophy

“It is the time to think how each one can address climate change. Energy is a key sector contributing to carbon. We create awareness for solar, wind and bio‑waste energy.”

The Cause

Chronic Foundation is a non‑profit organization focused on the “Green Energy” campaign, working to fast‑track zero‑carbon growth in urban communities.

Impact

The event combines fitness with environmental responsibility, and every step you take helps fund awareness sessions for renewable energy across Bengaluru.

Community

From Cubbon Park to the streets of Bengaluru, we bring together eco‑conscious athletes, families and volunteers to champion a healthier, greener city.
