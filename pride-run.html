About the Event

The Heartbeat of Mumbai

Mumbai's Silicon Valley swaps dress shoes for running gear, and comes together every year for a movement that brings a spring in the step of people from across the globe.

🏆

IAAF Gold Label

Among the youngest races to receive the prestigious IAAF Gold Label — a global recognition of excellence in road racing.

🌏

Global Participation

Runners from 30+ countries converge on Mumbai's iconic Marine Drive, making this a truly international celebration.

🌱

Sustainable by Design

Zero-plastic water stations, biodegradable bibs, and a carbon-offset program to make race day green.

❤️

Run for a Cause

Thousands run in support of partner NGOs. Over ₹5 crore raised for education and healthcare charities.

Common Questions

Registrations close on April 30, 2025 or when the category is full — whichever comes first. We strongly recommend registering early as popular categories like Open 10K sell out fast.
Bib collection will be held at the Mumbai Race Expo, Jio World Convention Centre, on May 16–17, 2025 (10 AM – 8 PM). Bring your registration confirmation and a valid photo ID. Same-day bib collection is not available.
Yes, complimentary baggage storage is available at the start village. Please use the provided baggage tag (included in your bib packet). The organizers are not responsible for lost or damaged items.
Transfers are allowed until April 15, 2025. Log into your account on our registration portal and use the "Transfer Registration" option. A small processing fee of ₹100 applies. Transfers after the deadline will not be processed.
Headphones are permitted for the Open 10K, Family Run, Senior Run, and Virtual Run. They are NOT allowed for the Elite World 10K in accordance with IAAF guidelines. We recommend bone-conduction headphones for safety on all categories.
