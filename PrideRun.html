Why We Run

Philosophy

"It is the time to think how each one can address climate change. Energy is a key sector contributing to carbon. We create awareness for solar, wind and bio‑waste energy."

Green Energy

Pride Run supports awareness for solar, wind and bio-waste solutions that can help Bengaluru move faster toward low-carbon growth.

Health

The event combines fitness with public education, encouraging people to care for both personal wellness and the planet.

Community

From Cubbon Park to the streets of Bengaluru, we bring together eco-conscious athletes, families and volunteers.
